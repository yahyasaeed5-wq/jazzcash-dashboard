📱 Install on iPhone
1
Use Safari on your iPhone — Chrome and Edge do not support installation on iOS.
2
Open this page, then tap the Share button (the box with an arrow ↑) at the bottom of the screen.
3
Scroll down in the Share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Confirm the name "JazzCash KPI" and tap Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen.
⚠️ The page must be served over https:// for Add to Home Screen to work — open the shared snapshot link from SharePoint, GitHub Pages, or any hosted URL. A local file:// path will not install.

Once installed, the app opens full-screen with no browser chrome, like a native app.
